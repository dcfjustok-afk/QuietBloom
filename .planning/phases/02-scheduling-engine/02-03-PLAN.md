By the end of 02-02, the scheduler boundary should already expose typed quiet-hours, pause-all, and reminder allowed-window inputs. This plan adds recovery-specific semantics on top of that boundary, especially:

```text
2-hour short-gap threshold
single catch-up occurrence per reminder
no historical replay batches
```

The UI may surface read-model state such as `Catch-up next`, `Paused until`, or `Quiet until`, but it must not add notification controls, history ledger views, settings pages, or snooze/skip/complete interactions.
